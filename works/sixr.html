SIXR.

In October of 2019 I started volunteering for SIXR, a nonprofit organization providing underrepresented communities of varying socio-cultural and economic background with early access to emerging technologies.

During my time there I had the opportunity to collaborate in different areas that went from creating documents with educational resources to sharing with others in workshops and teaching classes. Two of my most memorable experiences in this field were:

  • ChickTech workshop

    On January 25 of 2020 I was part of a 2 day seminar hosted by ChickTech where I taught a group of girls the basics of SparkAR so they could create their own AR filter for social media.

  • STEM Athlete

    In spring of 2020 SIXR started a program with 27 teenagers from Garfield High School teaching them the basics of Unity and coding, adding to the content of the regular classes. I was a teaching assistant during the 20 sessions of this program and helped with the questions that the students had during the classes.
