Interaction Design.

In 2016 I started studying Interaction Design. In this field, I learned that the best content you can create for people reflects their interests and needs. It was there where my interest in other methods of storytelling started. These are two projects that I developed during my career and I would like to highlight:

  • Civis - Virtual Reality game

    Civis is the result of an investigation that strives to generate a solution for the disrespectful driving in Bogot√°. After a series of interviews, it was found that the majority of people on the street see the problem in other people and not in themselves. For this reason, we decided to create a prototype of a virtual reality game which allows the user to put themselves in the place of another. Therefore, the user can experience how their actions can affect others.

  • QUAT - AR Experience

    QUAT is a hydrating candy for athletes designed by a group of Food Engineering students. Based on this product we created an AR experience that uses the package as the trigger of an AR Experience that shows the user a series of stretching routines to do post-exercise so that athletes can complement the hydration that the candy gives them with stretching and have an optimal recovery.
