Aesthetics.

Aesthetics is a collective that promotes art, music and culture in Bogotá. I started this collective with a group of friends in January of 2017 and created a community of more than 4.500 people that were part of the different events we promote. We decided to take a break from this project in 2020 but within those 3 years we brought DJ’s from all over the world to play at our events, enjoying a very good reception, always trying to create a space to promote emerging new artists from Colombia.
