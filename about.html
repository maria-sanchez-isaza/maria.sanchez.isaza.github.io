Hi, I am María.

I studied Interactive Design and Audiovisual and Multimedia Production in Bogotá, Colombia.

I'm passionate about creating immersive storytelling experiences. Here I can connect both of my careers: taking the spectator to the next level of immersion by changing their role from spectator to user, and sharing the message more effectively.

My work covers (amongst others) photography, video production, VR / AR / XR, web design, and UI / UX. I constantly expand my knowledge through a wide range of personal and professional interests.

For me, it is very important to help others with my work, supporting the development of individuals and communities. It’s important to me to create meaningful content that generates reflection for the people who interact with it.

I’m an organized and proactive person who enjoys teamwork and adapts easily. In my free time I like to be active, traveling, listening to music and playing sports.

Feel free to check my resume or contact me for more information.
